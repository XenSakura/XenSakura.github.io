About Me

Hi there,
I'm Sakura Shih. I'm a student currently at the DigiPen Institute of Technology.

I am a Systems/Tools Programmer with experience in Unity, Custom Game Engines, and in C/C++, and C#.

I am passionate about LGBTQIA+ representation within the game industry. I am currently the President of DigiPen's LGBTQIA+ student advocacy group (PRISM), and I work with administrative staff and faculty to organize events like DigiPen's involvement within the Seattle Pride March, Redmond Pride, and have coordinated involvement with queer organizations like Eastside Pride, Gay Gaming Professionals, and etc.

Outside of academics, I am passionate about music, photography, fitness, cooking, anime, and games.

My favorite music artists at the moment are Friday Pilots Club, South of France, and Battle Tapes. I generally like indie rock and indie pop.
